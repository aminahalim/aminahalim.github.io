Helping communities stay connected during a global pandemic.
My Roles: Opportunity Identification, Product Design





OPPORTUNITY
COVID-19 has shocked the world and caught most countries unprepared and extremely overwhelmed with trying to protect the health of their populations. However, amid all the confusion and the fear, the power of individuals, organizations, and communities to think differently and to innovate shows what can be achieved when people are united by common, clear priorities and necessity.





INSPIRATION
One of my neighbors came by my house a couple of weeks ago with homemade masks that she was distributing around our neighborhood. I started thinking about how quiet and disconnected our entire neighborhood had suddenly become. We have no knowledge about what our neighbors are going through during this time. Are they doing okay? Do they need any assistance? I left this conversation with my neighbor feeling inspired to start a new project.


PAIN POINTS
  • Forcing people to stay in their own homes leaves them stressed and anxious about what may be going on outside
  • People are unaware of how fast the virus is spreading in their neighborhood
  • People feel disconnected from their community
  • Elderly and immunocompromised people need help picking up essential items from the store



NEEDS
- A way to allow people see what is going on in their local community without having to go outside
- A way to see how fast the virus is spreading in their neighborhood
- A way to make people feel more connected to their community while confined in their own homes
- A way to help elderly and immunocompromised people feel less alone and helpless
