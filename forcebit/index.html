PROBLEM
Concussion related injuries account for over 275,000 hospitalizations every year. Concussion is a serious concern for athletes. 2 in 10 athletes suffer from concussions every year. Signs of concussion are often ignored, as there is no singular method to detect concussions in real time. 47% of all athletes feel no symptoms after a high-impact hit, causing them to neglect to report their injury to their medical personnel. When a concussion is left undiagnosed, it leads to increased chances of loss of consciousness in future concussions.






SOLUTION
ForceBit: A mouthguard that will assess the impact of force and acceleration on the head while athletes are playing on the field and transmit this data to a smartphone app where it can be monitored by concerned family members and coaches. This sports accessory is to be used in conjunction with existing screening procedures to detect possible concussions.
